Turnstyle

Turnstyle is a turn-based RPG with a (literal) twist! Rotate your party of 5 and learn to balance the stats of all your members in order to defeat the monsters of the subway.

I am a Character Concept and Asset Artist on the Turnstyle team. Play our demo now on Itch, check out our website for more info about the team and our development process, and follow us on Twitter for updates!
