About Me

Hey there! Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Mikayla, and I'm an artist specializing in character design, concept art, and 2D asset creation for games.

From paintings to jewelry to cosplay, I've been creating for as long as I can remember. I fell in love with the medium of video games at the age of 12 after playing The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess, and have dreamed of making games that can evoke equally powerful emotional responses in players ever since. Studying game design at UC Santa Cruz further deepened my adoration for games, and I'm looking forward to taking the next step in my journey towards fulfilling my childhood dreams.
