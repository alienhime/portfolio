Pinaccuracy



Mini Golf becomes extra thought-provoking when crossed with educational trivia! Pinaccuracy is an educational, multiplayer golf game made in Unity with a mobile framework.

I worked as a Concept Artist on this project, and also designed the game's logo, UI, and some promotional materials. Pinaccuracy is available to play now on Itch!

Character Design

I was responsible for designing the player character, as well the alien game show host, Wyzorbo. Both characters were then 3D modeled by another artist based off of my final character turnarounds.
